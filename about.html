Hello, I’m Yousef Abara

I’m 18 years old and from Syria. Ever since I was a child, I’ve been fascinated by electronics, PCs, and computer science. But because of the war, I never had the chance to fully explore or develop my talents in these fields. I lacked the time, the resources, and the stability that such learning requires.

Now that the war is over, I can finally start chasing my passion and learning the things I love. I currently study Computer Science at the Syrian Virtual University, which is especially convenient for me because it’s time-flexible — I can attend lectures whenever it suits me instead of being tied to fixed schedules.

Beyond the university, I’m also studying from other sources, such as CS50, which I’ve found to be a well-rounded course with excellent teachers and resources.

At this stage in my life, I don’t have a fixed plan for the future, but I have ambitions. My main goal is to keep learning, improving, and building the skills that will allow me to contribute meaningfully to the field of technology.
